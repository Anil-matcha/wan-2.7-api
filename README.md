# Wan 2.7: The Next Generation Video Foundation Model

**Wan 2.7** represents the latest evolution in Alibaba Cloud's state-of-the-art video generation series, succeeding the groundbreaking **Wan 2.6**. 

Building upon the "Reference-to-Video" (R2V) and narrative capabilities of its predecessor, Wan 2.7 pushes the boundaries of cinematic AI, visual consistency, and long-form storytelling.

---

## Evolution: From Wan 2.6 to Wan 2.7

### Previous Model: Wan 2.6 (December 2025)
Wan 2.6 was a major milestone that moved beyond simple clips to professional-grade production:
- **Reference-to-Video (R2V):** Allowed users to maintain character and object consistency across different generations by providing a visual reference.
- **Narrative Depth:** Introduced "structured prompting" to generate multi-shot 15-second clips with complex scene transitions.
- **Audio-Visual Sync:** Native support for lip-syncing and synchronized sound effects.
- **1080p Quality:** Standardized high-definition output for professional social media and marketing use.

### Next Generation: Wan 2.7 (Early 2026)
Wan 2.7 refines the core architecture of 2.6, introducing several key "next-gen" improvements:

#### 1. Enhanced Temporal Stability
Wan 2.7 utilizes an updated **3D Causal VAE (v3)** that significantly reduces motion blur and "warping" in complex action sequences, such as fast-moving sports or intricate hand gestures.

#### 2. Advanced Character Persistence
While 2.6 introduced R2V, Wan 2.7 features **Global Character Embedding**. This allows the model to remember and replicate a character's features across multiple separate video files, facilitating the creation of entire short films with a consistent cast.

#### 3. Expanded Narrative Windows
The model can now handle even more complex structured prompts, effectively doubling the narrative complexity. It can manage up to **30 seconds** of continuous, high-fidelity video in a single generation pass, maintaining coherence from start to finish.

#### 4. Improved Spatial Awareness
Wan 2.7 understands 3D spatial relationships better than ever. This results in more realistic lighting interactions, accurate shadows, and a better "sense of depth" when the camera pans or zooms through a scene.

#### 5. Native Multimodal Editing
Beyond just generation, Wan 2.7 supports **Instruction-Based Video Editing**. Users can take an existing video and provide natural language instructions like *"Change the character's jacket to leather"* or *"Make the scene look like it's raining,"* and the model will perform the edit while preserving the original motion and structure.
